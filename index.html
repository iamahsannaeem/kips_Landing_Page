Our Success Story
KIPS is not merely an educational institute, it is a phenomenon. Over the last three decades, KIPS has earned outstanding success through its extensive network of Schools, Colleges, Evening Coaching, Entry Test Preparation, Competitive Exam Preparation, Foreign Test Preparation, School of Languages, and Publications . It has a league of successful students and professionals throughout the country and beyond. An immensely competent faculty, purpose-built campuses with state-of the-art facilities, and an elaborate quality assurance system are some of the ingredients of KIPS’ success.
